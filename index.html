Who We Are

About Us

Our journey began with a collective of architects and engineers driven by a singular purpose: to redefine modern living through sustainable design in apartments, residences, and condominiums. At the heart of this movement is Architect Cristher Dacome, whose personal history of struggle and inspiration paved the way for this new era. He founded DACS not merely as a business, but as a declaration of faith — honoring God's unwavering faithfulness and recognizing the divine provision that continues to sustain this mission.

Your hard work deserves a home that lasts. We offer expert design-build solutions that take you from 'scratch' to 'reality,' creating spaces where you can truly enjoy the life you've built.
